Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using JantaReport:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and city of residence
  • Issue Reports: Descriptions, images, videos, and GPS coordinates of reported issues
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, browser type, and interaction patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and route civic issue reports to appropriate authorities
  • Provide real-time updates on issue resolution status
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Ensure accountability and transparency in governance
  • Prevent fraud and maintain platform security

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information only with:

  • Government Agencies: Municipal authorities and relevant departments for issue resolution
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in platform operations (under strict confidentiality)

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with regular backups
  • Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Lodge a complaint with data protection authorities

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at:
